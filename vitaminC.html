Vitamins performs hundreds of roles in our body by converting the food into energy.

Things you need to know about Vitamin C

Vitamin C is also known as ascorbic acid. It is one of the safest and most effective nutrients.It has many free radical molecules which are much beneficial for our body. A recent study says that there are over 100 studies over 10 years on growing list of vitamin C.



Benefits of Vitamin C

Vitamin C performs various functions in our body also helps in many ways.

  • It has collagen which cures wounds
  • helping to protect various cells and tissues
  • maintaining healthy skin and blood vessels
  • It is helpful in eye diseases
  • It protect us from cancer, stroke, anemia.

Sources of Vitamin C

  • oranges and other citrus fruits
  • strawberries
  • brocolli
  • potatoes
  • green and red peppers
  • tomato and tomato juice
  • lemon juice
  • green leafy vegetables

Intake values of Vitamin C

  • upto 3 years: 15 miligram(mg) per day
  • 4 to 8 years: 25 miligram(mg) per day
  • 9 to 13 years: 45 miligram(mg) per day
  • 14 to 19 years: 75 miligram(mg) per day
  • 19+ years: 90 miligram(mg) per day

Risk of over consuming

Taking too much of vitamin C is unlikely to cause any significant problems, but if a person consumes more than 1,000 mg of it they will not absorb it at all.

Moreover the high level or the over consumption of vitamin C may cause problem of diarrhea and intestinal discomfort. Also the high level of supplements may result in problem of kidney stones. So it is recommended not to over consume it.
